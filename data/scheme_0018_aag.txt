### Description
&amp;quot;Aponar Apon Ghar&amp;quot; is a Home Loan Subsidy Scheme by the Finance Department, Govt. of Assam. The objective is to realize the vision of “Housing For All” by 2022 so that each poor person may have their own house. All the applicants will get home loans at subsidized interest rates. Applicant must avail of housing loans from any Scheduled Commercial Bank, Regional Rural Bank, or Assam Cooperative Apex bank within the state. The state govt. will provide ₹ 2,50,000 subsidy on home loans up to ₹ 40,00,000. This house loan subsidy is only for those who are purchasing their 1st house and have not availed of loans under the previous Apun Ghar Scheme. The scope of the scheme is limited to the permanent residents of the state of Assam.

Subsidy (₹): 2,00,000
Loan Amount (₹): 30,00,000 - 40,00,000
Subsidy (₹): 2,50,000

### Eligibility

1. The applicants must be permanent residents of Assam state.
1. The applicants must avail the housing loan from any Scheduled Commercial Bank, Regional Rural Banks, Assam Cooperative Apex bank within the state.
1. The total family income of the applicant (from all sources) must not exceed ₹ 20,00,000.
1. The housing loan must be of more than ₹ 5,00,000 and sanctioned by bank on or after 1st April 2019.
1. The loan accounts must not be under NPA (Non Performing Assets) status.
1. This must be the first home by the composite family.
