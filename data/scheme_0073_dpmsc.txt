### Scheme Name
Devnarayan Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme

### Short Title
DPMSC

### Level
State

### Category
Education & Learning

### Target Beneficiaries
Individual

### Tags
Education, Scholarship, Financial Assistance, Student

### Description
Extremely Backward Class Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme has been implemented on the lines of the pre-matric scholarship scheme being given to Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe students for classes 6th to 10th of very backward classes.


### Benefits
Selected students have to study according to the approved syllabus by State Government.
As per the rules  Education,  Accommodation, Meal,  Dress,  text writing material and books will be provided Free of cost.

### Eligibility

1. The students should be a native of Rajasthan state.
1. Applicant students should belong to the following Other Backward Classes, that must have been issued by Tehsildar or a higher-level revenue officer.
(i). Banjara, Baladiya, Labana. 
(ii). Gadia-Lohar, Gadolia.
(iii). Gujar, Gurjar. 
(iv). Raika, Raibari (Debasi).
(v). Gadariya (Gadri), Gayari.
1.  The annual income of the parents/guardian of the applicant from all sources (including the candidate&#39;s own income, if any) should be up to Rs.2,00,000 (Rupees two lakh). 
1. An employed student who studies as a full-time student by taking leave without pay during the entire course will be eligible for the scholarship.
1. All the children of the same parent/guardian will be eligible to benefit from this scheme.
1. Students who are not getting any kind of scholarship or allowance for study from central, state / public sources.
