### Scheme Name
Usha Kiran Yojana

### Short Title
UKY

### Level
State

### Category
Housing & Shelter

### Target Beneficiaries
Individual

### Tags
Physical Abuse, Sexual Abuse, Mental Abuse, Deprivation Of Liberty, Domestic Violence

### Description
Violence against women and their children below the age of 18 years by a family member/members, which includes physical, sexual, mental, emotional, economic, threats or coercive or arbitrary deprivation of liberty, whether at home or in a public place, comes under the category of domestic violence. 
For all these protection and assistance, the state government has started the &quot;Usha Kiran Yojana&quot; to provide protection and assistance against domestic violence. To implement the Women&#39;s Protection from Domestic Violence Act 2005, Rule 2006.


### Benefits
Providing protection and support against domestic violence

### Eligibility

1. All women of all age groups.
1. All children under the age of 18.



### Application Process
Mode: Offline
Applicants have to visit the following centers
Protection Officer (Project Officer-Integrated Child Development Project Office).
One Stop Center operated in the district.
Police Station.
